Route

From Enniskillen, go to the end of the main street in Derrygonnelly:

Turn left at the Big Yellow Arrow. Note: the Big Yellow Arrow will not be visible when you’re there. If it is, you’ve got problems - turn around and go home.

Follow the road for about 10km.

You’ll be travelling left-to-right along the bottom of the hill (Knockmore) in the distance.

At the end of this road is a turn to the right: that’s where you’ll be parking:

Here’s the turn-off:

Parking is 50m from here. Even though the board at the car park says “Big Dog Walk”, you're actually going onto Little Dog.
