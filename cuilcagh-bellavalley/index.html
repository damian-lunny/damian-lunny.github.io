Route

To get to the starting point from Enniskillen, drive to Swanlinbar.

At the end of the street, in front of The Stables pub, turn right:

Follow the road for about 6 miles (along the way, you'll pass Tullydermott waterfall), until you come to the Blackcross junction - take a right onto the R200:

After 1/1.5 miles, you'll come onto a longish straight - you'll be taking the left turn at the top of this straight followed by a quick right (where you'll park) (in the picture below, I've tried to include the Bellavalley Gap road sign on the extreme left):

The next picture is immediately after the straight in the previous picture - there should be plenty of room to park. If not, keep going for another 100 yards: there's another car park there.

After parking, follow the gravel path to the top. You'll come to a mobile phone mast. Cross the fence and follow the path up the hill. At the top, turn left towards the lone fence-post. Follow the path to the summit.

This picture is an overhead shot of the route from the car park - you'll follow the zig-zag to the little white patch (that's the mobile phone mast):

There are five R200 boxes shown in that overhead shot - the car park (and the start of the walk up) is between the fourth and fifth. The second car park, should you need it, is beside the third R200 box.
